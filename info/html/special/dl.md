# Definition

[Go back](..)

You can use **dl** and **dt**/**dd** to make some 
dictionary-like style

```html
<dl>
    <dt>a word</dt>
    <dd>a definition here</dd>
</dl>
```

<div class="sr"></div>

According to the renderer, the definition block may
be indented or the word may be in bold+italic.
