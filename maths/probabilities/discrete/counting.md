Ordered and unordered sets are syntactically different:

* Pick one and two: $\lbrace (1, 2),(2, 1) \rbrace$ <small>(ordered set)</small>
* Pick one and two: $\lbrace \lbrace{1,2}\rbrace\rbrace$ <small>(unordered set)</small>
