#### Ordered distribution Example

How many words can we create with the letters of Mississippi?

* we got 1 M ($o_1$)
* we got 4 I ($o_2$)
* we got 4 S ($o_3$)
* we got 2 P ($o_4$)
* we got 11 letters

@
\frac{11!}{1! * 4! * 4! * 2!} = 34650
@
