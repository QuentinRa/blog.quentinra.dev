Table

tabemasu = manger
	* Question : nihon no tabemono nani ga suki desu ka?

nomimasu = boire
	* Question : nani o nomimasu ka ?
	* Rep : <truc> o nomimasu

asagohan = petit déjeuner
	* Question : asagohan o tabemasuka?
	* Rep : hai, <truc> tabemasu
	* Rep : iee, asagohan o tabemasen
	* Question : asagohan itsumo nani o tabemasuka?
	* Rep: Watashi wa <truc> o tabemasu.
